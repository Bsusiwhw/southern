About Our Company

Introduction

Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. is a reputable ISO/IEC 17025:2017 accredited calibration laboratory, located at Artica Building, National Highway corner Almazora Street, Canlalay, Binan City, Laguna. Established in 2015, we have quickly become a trusted name in the industry, offering comprehensive metrology and calibration services to customers across various sectors. As the calibration laboratory in the Philippines to be accredited by the Philippine Accreditation Bureau for ISO/IEC 17025:2017, we have a track record of delivering exceptional quality and accuracy. With our customer-centric approach, state-of-the-art facilities, and a highly knowledgeable staff, we guarantee top-notch performance and professional quality services.

Services

We specialize in providing precise and reliable metrology and calibration services tailored to the specific needs of our customers. With a wide scope of accreditation, we offer calibration services in diverse fields, including thermometry, hydrometry, mass, balances, force, pressure, torque, analytical, environmental, volume, dimensional, electrical, frequency, density, and viscosity. Our services ensure that your measurement procedures meet the highest quality standards, providing you with confidence and accuracy in your processes.

Facilities and Equipment

Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. is proud to possess the largest private laboratory facility in the country. With a spacious and well-equipped facility spanning a laboratory area of 1200 square meters across three floors, we have the capacity to handle calibration projects of any size and complexity. Our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with cutting-edge equipment, ensuring accurate and precise measurements across various calibration fields.

Personnel and Expertise

Our team comprises 90 highly skilled personnel who have undergone extensive training both domestically and abroad. They possess in-depth knowledge and expertise in their respective calibration fields. Our personnel have received training from prestigious institutions, including the National Metrology Institutes, and are recognized as experts and assessors of ISO/IEC 17025:2017. This ensures that our calibration services adhere to the highest industry standards. Additionally, our personnel undergo continuous training to stay updated with the latest advancements in metrology and calibration practices.

Accreditations and Compliance

We have gained recognition for our commitment to quality and compliance with regulatory bodies. As a calibration laboratory to receive accreditation from the Philippine Accreditation Bureau for ISO/IEC 17025:2017, we have set a benchmark for excellence in the industry. Our compliance with regulatory requirements ensures that our calibration services meet international standards and best practices. With a total of 15 approved signatories, we have a team of experts who oversee and certify the accuracy and reliability of our calibration processes.

Additional Services

In addition to our core calibration services, we offer additional services to support our customers' needs. These include training on management systems and calibration to enhance your understanding of industry standards and procedures. We also serve as a proficiency testing provider, enabling you to assess the competence and accuracy of your measurement processes. Furthermore, we provide inspection and certification services for advanced biosafety cabinets, fume hoods, microscopes, as well as thermal and relative humidity validation.

Contact Us

We encourage you to explore our website to learn more about Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc., our comprehensive range of calibration fields, and the additional services we offer. If you have any questions or inquiries, please feel free to contact us at any time. Our dedicated team is ready to assist you and provide expert guidance in metrology and calibration.
